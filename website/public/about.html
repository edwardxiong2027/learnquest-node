About LearnQuest

Built by students, for students. Making quality education free, offline, and accessible to all.

What Drives Us

Every child deserves a patient, knowledgeable tutor available anytime, anywhere.

Our Mission

Make quality education free and accessible to every student

We believe that a student's ZIP code, internet access, or family income should never determine the quality of education they receive. LearnQuest delivers a complete K-8 learning platform that runs entirely offline on any computer, ensuring no student is left behind.

Our Vision

A world where every child has a personal tutor in their pocket

We envision a future where AI-powered education tools are as common as textbooks, where students in rural communities and underserved schools have the same learning support as those in well-funded districts. LearnQuest is our first step toward that future.

Our Story

From a high school club idea to an open-source platform serving students everywhere.

Fall 2025

The Spark

The Vibe Coding Club at Diamond Bar High School asked a simple question: what if we could build a free tutoring platform that works without internet? Many students in our community and beyond lack reliable broadband access, yet deserve the same learning opportunities.

Winter 2025

Building the Foundation

We chose a lightweight AI model (Phi-3 Mini) that runs locally, combined with computationally-verified math accuracy. We wrote hundreds of lessons across Math, Science, ELA, and Social Studies aligned to Common Core standards. The platform took shape on a USB drive.

Spring 2026

Going Open Source

We released LearnQuest as a free, open-source project on GitHub so that teachers, developers, and communities around the world can use, improve, and share it. The response from educators has been inspiring.

Now & Beyond

Growing Together

We're partnering with schools, nonprofits, and educational foundations to distribute LearnQuest to students who need it most. Our roadmap includes more subjects, more languages, and more ways for the community to contribute.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we build.

🌐

Accessibility

Education should be available to everyone, regardless of internet access, location, or economic status. Offline-first, always free.

🎯

Accuracy

Every math answer is verified computationally, not by AI. We never guess. Students deserve correct answers and reliable explanations.

🔒

Privacy

Zero data collection. No tracking. No accounts sent to servers. All student data stays on the local device, period.

🤝

Community

Open source means everyone can contribute. Teachers add content, developers fix bugs, and students shape the future of learning.
