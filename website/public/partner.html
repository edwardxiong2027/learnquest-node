The Problem We're Solving

Millions of students lack access to quality educational support. The digital divide is real.

16M+ U.S. students lack reliable home internet access, limiting their ability to use online learning tools
$40-80/hr The average cost of private tutoring, putting personalized learning support out of reach for most families
1 in 3 Rural students lack broadband access at home, creating a persistent gap in educational opportunity

Our Solution

LearnQuest is a free, open-source tutoring platform that runs entirely offline.

LearnQuest delivers a complete K-8 curriculum across Math, Science, ELA, and Social Studies with an AI tutor that runs locally on any computer. No internet required after setup. No subscription fees. No data collection. Just a student, a computer, and a world of learning.

Our dedicated math engine ensures 100% computational accuracy for every math problem. The AI explains concepts in student-friendly language while the math engine verifies every answer. Students earn XP, unlock badges, and track their progress through a gamified experience that makes learning fun.
